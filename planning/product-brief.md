# MACROmate Product Brief

## Problem

Nutrition tracking apps help individuals log what they have eaten or are about to eat, but it's not always easy to plan out daily or future intake to meet individual macro goals.

## Solution

MACROmate helps users meet nutrition goals by allowing them to plan daily intake from saved pantry foods, showing remaining daily calories and macros, and how much of each item can be consumed before meeting or exceeting their set goals. 

## Target User

Individual who track their macros and want a faster way to plan daily meals around their favorite foods.

## Core Value

No more asking "what have I already eaten?", MACROmate answers the question "what can I still eat?"

## MVP Features

- Set daily calories and macro targets
- Save commonly used foods to "My Pantry"
- Add foods from "My Pantry" into "Today's Meals"
- View current daily macro and calorie intake totals
- View remaining macros needed to meet targets
- See estimated maximum amounts of each "My Pantry" food that still fits remaining targets

## Out of Scope for MVP

- User authentication
- Backend database
- Barcode scanner
- AI meal generator
- Social media features
- Wearable integrations
- Micronutrient tracking

## Success Criteria

The user can open the app, add foods to "My Pantry," select foods in specified quantities to add into "Today's Meals," and immediately see updated remaining macros to meet set targets.
